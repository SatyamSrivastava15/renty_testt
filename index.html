About RENTY

A 2026 startup born from a simple dream — make quality vehicle rentals accessible to everyone in Ranchi.

RENTY is a startup company launched in 2026 by Satyam Srivastava, CEO & Founder. Located at Mahavir Chowk, Upper Bazar, Ranchi, Jharkhand, RENTY offers a curated fleet of cars, bikes, and scooties — all in good condition at minimum pricing.

We believe every journey should begin on your terms. Whether it's a quick city ride or an adventurous weekend trip, RENTY has the right vehicle waiting for you.
